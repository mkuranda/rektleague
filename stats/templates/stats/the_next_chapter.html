YOU THOUGHT WE WERE DONE?

In January of 2021, we felt that the Rekt League had run its course, and we declared an end to it. Over the course of seven seasons, the league had changed into something we were no longer excited to work on, and we were itching for something new. We missed the lower stakes of earlier seasons, when practice wasn't required and hype videos were commonplace. And League of Legends was fun, but we wanted more.
